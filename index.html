Favorite Authors

My favorite book genre is fiction. They offer adventure, deep characters, and immersive worlds. Here are a few of my favorite authors and why I love them:

  1. Brandon Sanderson
    • Incredible worldbuilding
    • The Cosmere universe is a masterpiece!
    • The Stormlight Archive has great deep and applicable teachings
  2. Robert Jordan
    • The Wheel of Time is epic in scale and scope
    • The most fun I've ever had reading a series before.
    • Quick wit, attentive, packed with a punch
  3. Rick Riordan
    • Modern myths and relatable characters
    • One of few authors that can make me laugh out loud with his writing
  4. Jane Austen
    • High level wit
    • Clever yet relatable whirlwind romance
  5. Shirtaloon
    • He Who Fights With Monsters series is a delightful LitRPG ride
    • Funny and emotional in one.
